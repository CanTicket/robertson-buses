# Buses - User Training Guide

## **For Drivers/Staff**

### **Getting Started**

1. **Login**
   - Navigate to: `https://buses.yourdomain.com`
   - Enter your email and password (provided by admin)
   - Click "Login"

2. **Dashboard Overview**
   - **Today's Shift**: Shows if you're currently clocked in
   - **Pending Tasks**: Any incomplete actions
   - **Quick Actions**: Clock in/out, checklist, leave request

---

### **Clocking In for Your Shift**

1. Click **"Clock In"** button on dashboard
2. System records your start time automatically
3. Green status bar appears: "Currently Clocked In"
4. **Important:** You'll need to complete a checklist before clocking out

---

### **Completing End-of-Day Safety Checklist**

**‚ö†Ô∏è MANDATORY: You cannot clock out without completing this checklist**

#### **Step-by-Step Process:**

**1. Access Checklist**
   - Near end of shift, click **"Complete Checklist"** button
   - Or navigate to: Menu ‚Üí Checklist ‚Üí Create

**2. Select Vehicle**
   - Choose the bus you drove today from dropdown
   - Example: "Bus 101 (ABC123)"

**3. Tyre Inspection**
   - **Front Tyres**: Select Good / Fair / Poor
   - **Rear Tyres**: Select Good / Fair / Poor
   - **Tip:** "Good" = Normal wear, no visible damage

**4. Fuel Level**
   - Move the slider to indicate current fuel percentage
   - Example: 75% = Three-quarters full

**5. CRITICAL: Kids Check**
   - **Question:** "Are there any kids left on the bus?"
   - **Select "No"** if all children accounted for
   - **Select "Yes"** ONLY if kids remain on bus
     - ‚ö†Ô∏è Selecting "Yes" sends immediate CRITICAL ALERT to all managers
     - Only use if genuinely required

**6. Upload Photos (Optional)**
   - Take photos of any damage, issues, or concerns
   - Click "Choose Files" and select up to 5 images
   - Accepted: JPG, PNG (max 5MB each)

**7. Additional Notes (Optional)**
   - Type any observations, issues, or comments
   - Examples:
     - "Strange noise from engine"
     - "Minor scratch on left side"
     - "Low windshield washer fluid"

**8. Submit Checklist**
   - Review all entries
   - Click **"Complete Checklist"**
   - ‚úÖ Success message appears
   - You can now clock out

---

### **Clocking Out**

1. Once checklist is completed, click **"Clock Out"** button
2. System calculates your total hours
3. Shift ends, timesheet auto-generated

**Note:** If you try to clock out without completing checklist, you'll see:
> "Please complete your end-of-day safety checklist before clocking out."

---

### **Viewing Your Checklist History**

1. Menu ‚Üí Checklists ‚Üí My Checklists
2. See list of all completed checklists
3. Click any checklist to view details
4. Shows:
   - Date and time
   - Vehicle used
   - Inspection results
   - Manager approval status

---

### **Requesting Leave**

1. Menu ‚Üí Leave ‚Üí Request Leave
2. Fill in form:
   - **Type:** Annual Leave, Sick Leave, Personal Leave
   - **Date:** Select day(s) needed
   - **Hours:** Number of hours (e.g., 8 for full day)
   - **Reason:** Brief explanation
3. Click **"Submit Request"**
4. Manager will approve/decline
5. Check status: Menu ‚Üí Leave ‚Üí My Requests

---

### **Submitting Timesheets**

1. Menu ‚Üí Timesheets ‚Üí Submit Timesheet
2. Select date range (usually weekly)
3. Review hours (auto-populated from clock in/out)
4. Add any notes if needed
5. Click **"Submit for Approval"**
6. Manager reviews and approves

---

### **Troubleshooting**

**Problem:** Can't clock out
- **Solution:** Complete your safety checklist first

**Problem:** Forgot to clock in
- **Solution:** Contact your manager to manually add your shift

**Problem:** Made mistake in checklist
- **Solution:** Contact manager immediately to flag and correct

**Problem:** Photo won't upload
- **Solution:** Ensure image is JPG/PNG and under 5MB

### **Reviewing Safety Checklists**

**1. Access Review Queue**
   - Menu ‚Üí Checklists ‚Üí Review
   - Or click "X Pending Reviews" on dashboard

**2. View Checklist Card**
   - Shows: Bus number, driver name, completion time
   - Quick summary of inspection results
   - Status badges (Good ‚úÖ / Issue ‚ö†Ô∏è / Alert üö®)

**3. Open Full Checklist**
   - Click **"Review Checklist"** button
   - See detailed inspection results
   - View uploaded photos
   - Read driver notes

**4. Review Actions**

**Option A: Approve**
- All checks look good
- No issues requiring follow-up
- Add optional review notes
- Click **"Approve Checklist"**
- Driver notified of approval

**Option B: Flag for Follow-Up**
- Issues identified requiring action
- Examples:
  - "Poor" tyre condition noted
  - Damage photo shows concern
  - Fuel level critically low
- Add **required** notes explaining why flagged
- Click **"Flag for Follow-Up"**
- Admin notified, checklist marked red

---

### **Handling Kids Left Alert üö®**

**CRITICAL SAFETY PROTOCOL**

**1. Alert Received**
   - Email: "CRITICAL ALERT: Kids Left on Bus"
   - In-app notification badge
   - Checklist shows red border

**2. Immediate Actions**
   - **VERIFY IMMEDIATELY:** Contact driver
   - Check vehicle location
   - Dispatch staff to bus if needed
   - Ensure all children accounted for

**3. Document Resolution**
   - Open flagged checklist
   - Add detailed review notes:
     - Actions taken
     - Outcome/resolution
     - Time resolved
   - Flag checklist with notes for admin review

**4. Follow-Up**
   - Report incident to admin
   - Driver counseling if false alarm
   - Review procedures if genuine

## **For Administrators**

### **Fleet Management**

**Adding New Vehicle:**
1. Menu ‚Üí Fleet ‚Üí Vehicles
2. Click **"Add New Vehicle"**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Bus Number:** Display name (e.g., "Bus 101")
   - **Registration:** License plate (e.g., "ABC123")
   - **Make/Model:** Mercedes-Benz Sprinter
   - **Year:** 2023
   - **Capacity:** 45 passengers
   - **Status:** Active
4. Click **"Add Vehicle"**

**Editing Vehicle:**
- Click pencil icon next to vehicle
- Update fields as needed
- Save changes

**Deactivating Vehicle:**
- Edit vehicle
- Change Status to "Maintenance" or "Inactive"
- Prevents selection in checklists

**Viewing Vehicle History:**
- Click eye icon next to vehicle
- See all checklists for that bus
- Identify recurring issues

---

### **User Management**

1. Menu ‚Üí Users ‚Üí Add User

**Deactivating User:**
- Users ‚Üí Find user ‚Üí Set Status to "Inactive"

## **FAQ**

**Q: What if I forget to complete my checklist?**
A: You won't be able to clock out. Complete it ASAP or contact your manager.

**Q: Can I edit a checklist after submission?**
A: No. Contact your manager if you made an error.

**Q: How long are checklists stored?**
A: Indefinitely for compliance purposes. Archived after 90 days.

**Q: What happens if I select "Yes" for kids left on bus?**
A: CRITICAL ALERT sent to all managers immediately. Only use if genuine.

**Q: Can I complete checklist from my phone?**
A: Yes! The website is mobile-responsive. Use any browser.

**Q: Who can see my checklists?**
A: You, your managers, and administrators. Not other drivers.
