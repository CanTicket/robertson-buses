## End-of-Day Safety Checklist (Core New Feature)

### Checklist Flow:

1. **Driver clocks in** ‚Üí System assigns vehicle/bus
2. **During shift** ‚Üí Normal time tracking
3. **Before clocking out** ‚Üí **MANDATORY CHECKLIST**

- Tyre condition (Front/Rear - Good/Fair/Poor)
- Fuel level (% or liters remaining)
- Interior check: "Kids left on bus?" (Yes/No with alert)
- Exterior condition: Photo upload
- Notes field for any issues

4. **Manager review** ‚Üí Approve or flag for follow-up
5. **Reports** ‚Üí Checklist completion tracking

### Safety Logic:

- ‚ùå **Cannot clock out until checklist is completed**
- üö® If "Kids left" = Yes ‚Üí Immediate alert to manager
- üì∏ Photo uploads stored securely (Dreamscape storage)
- üìä Checklist history for compliance audits

## Technical Architecture

### Database Schema

**From CanTicket (Reused):**

- `users` - Staff records
- `user_access` - Role definitions
- `task_timer` ‚Üí **`shift_timer`** (renamed for buses)
- `leaves` - Leave management
- `team_schedule` ‚Üí **`staff_shifts`** (renamed)
- Notification, reporting, and permission tables

**NEW for Buses:**

- `vehicles` - Basic bus registry
- vehicle_id, registration_number, bus_number, status, company_id
- `daily_checklists` - Safety checks
- checklist_id, shift_timer_id, vehicle_id, user_id, completed_at
- `checklist_items` - Individual check results
- item_id, checklist_id, check_type, value, notes
- `checklist_photos` - Image uploads
- photo_id, checklist_id, photo_path, uploaded_at

### Technology Stack

**Backend:** Laravel 11.x, MySQL 8.0+
**Frontend:** Blade, Tailwind CSS, Alpine.js
**Hosting:** **Dreamscape** (shared hosting or VPS)
**Storage:** Dreamscape file storage (checklist photos)
**Notifications:** Laravel Mail (SMTP via Dreamscape)
